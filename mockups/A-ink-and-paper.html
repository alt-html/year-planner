A — "Ink & Paper"

Direction: Editorial warmth. The grid is the hero — everything else recedes.

  • Typography: Playfair Display (serif) for headlines and months — gives the feel of a printed wall calendar. Source Sans 3 for body — clean and readable at small sizes.
  • Palette: Warm cream paper (#FAF7F2) with ink-brown text (#2C2520). Terracotta accent (#D4663A) on the year and interactive highlights. Amber today marker (#E8A838).
  • Nav: Single thin strip — title + year arrows left, tool icons right. No dropdown menus visible by default. The "Year" dropdown becomes simple chevron arrows. Menu items collapse into a vertical ellipsis.
  • Weekends: Warm blush (#F7F0E8) instead of cold aliceblue — harmonises with the cream base.
  • Today: Amber circle on the day number — immediately scannable without overwhelming the grid.
  • Dark mode: Stone-dark (#1C1917) with warm grey text — cozy, not clinical.
