C — "Verdant Studio"

Direction: Organic warmth meets studio-tool structure.

  • Typography: Fraunces (variable serif) for headlines — warm, characterful, contemporary. Outfit for body — geometric clarity without being cold.
  • Palette: Deep forest green (#2D5A3D) as the brand colour. Warm stone background (#F5F2ED). Burnt orange accent (#C85A28) for today and actions.
  • Nav: Vertical icon rail on the left edge — inspired by VS Code, Figma, and modern creative tools. Title and year navigation in a thin header bar.
  • Grid: Full-width canvas. Green month headers create a strong horizontal reading line.
  • Today: Burnt orange circle — vibrant against the green-stone palette.
  • Dark mode: Deep forest (#141C16) — green undertone makes it feel lush.
