B — "Nordic Clarity"

Direction: Scandinavian minimalism with bold precision accents.

  • Typography: DM Sans — geometric and warm but not generic. JetBrains Mono for the day-of-week column — gives it a structured, engineered feel. Abbreviated day/month names save space and look cleaner.
  • Palette: Cool off-white (#F8F9FB) with pure white grid on top. Dark header band (#1A1D23) gives the months strong visual weight. Electric blue accent (#2563EB) for today and interactive elements.
  • Nav: Floating pill toolbar centred at top — like a macOS toolbar or Figma's floating UI. Year navigation with chevrons, tools as icon-only buttons. The whole bar is self-contained and feels modern.
  • Grid: Rounded corners, subtle shadow — feels like a card, not a spreadsheet. Dark header row creates clear hierarchy.
  • Today: Blue rounded-square badge on the day number — consistent with the rounded aesthetic.
  • Dark mode: Deep navy-black (#0D0F14) — feels like an IDE or pro tool, not just an inversion.
